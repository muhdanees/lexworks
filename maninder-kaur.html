Maninder Kaur

Position: Senior Associate

Education: B.Sc., LL.B., LL.M.

Maninder Kaur is a dedicated Senior Associate at Lex Works, specializing in indirect taxation and patent law. With a strong academic foundation that includes a B.Sc., an LL.B., and an LL.M., she brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the team.

Since joining the firm, Maninder Kaur has focused on navigating the complexities of indirect tax regulations and intellectual property rights, particularly in the area of patents. She has successfully managed numerous cases, providing clients with strategic advice and representation in both contentious and non-contentious matters.

Known for her meticulous approach and strong analytical skills, Maninder Kaur excels in developing tailored strategies that align with clients' business objectives while ensuring compliance with legal standards. She is committed to building lasting relationships with clients, understanding their unique needs, and advocating effectively on their behalf. She is also dedicated to continuous professional development, actively participating in relevant workshops and seminars to stay current in the evolving fields of taxation and intellectual property law.

At Lex Works, Maninder Kaur is focused on contributing to the firm's growth and success, leveraging her expertise to help clients navigate the complexities of indirect taxation and patent law with confidence.
